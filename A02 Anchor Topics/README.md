# A02 Anchor Topics

> **Entry points for your thinking. MOCs (Maps of Content) per theme.**

## What goes here

An **Anchor Topic** is an index note about a theme that YOU built, with:
- Your thesis/position on the theme
- Links to child notes (permanent notes in B03 or A02)
- Open questions
- Relevant external references

It is the "entry door" to navigate your second brain.

## Possible anchor examples

- `Observability.md` — everything you know about observability (links to blindpay, abacatepay notes, concepts)
- `Payments.md` — overview of payments (Pix, cards, etc.)
- `Zettelkasten.md` — your theory of note construction
- `Remote team management.md`

## Structure (use template)

Use `C04 Claude Obsidian/06 Templates & References/template-anchor-topic.md`

## Difference from B03 Resources

- **A02 Anchor** = high-level MOC, authored reflection
- **B03 Resource** = specific note, reference material

## Rules

1. **Authorship is yours.** I (Claude) never write an anchor. I can suggest structure or links.
2. Each anchor has a clear thesis.
3. Bidirectional links — the anchor points to its children, the children point to the anchor.
4. Quarterly review — is the thesis still yours?

## How to create

Invoke `/create-anchor` with the topic. I propose structure from the template and related notes I find in the vault.
